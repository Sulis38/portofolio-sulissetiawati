In the material computation subject, I conducted computations using Lumerical FDTD software to modify the Tunable Graphene Metamaterial Absorber. The objective of this modification was to achieve an increased absorption range. The modification involved replacing Topas Polymer with Al2O3, altering the geometry, and analyzing the influence of the layer's thickness. As a result, a 9% increase in absorption bandwidth was obtained, and the thickness was found to have no significant effect.
